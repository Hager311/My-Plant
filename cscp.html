My Plant

Your own Space for growing plants

This website connects you with other young people who share your interests, teaching you how to grow and track your plants from seed.
Explore our virtual greenhouse and find the perfect plants to improve your home or work environment. With the right care and conditions, these resilient species will reward you with colorful blooms and peace of mind.

Our plants are divided into four different groups to help you find the ones that will improve your area and make it more comfortable:

You can find the ways on how to grow the trees. As the roots of a tree are very important. The roots go deep into the ground and hold the tree in place. The roots also get water and nutrients from the soil and send them up into the rest of the tree. Roots also store food for the tree.
When you plant a tree, it's important to take good care of the roots. You need to prepare the soil well and water the tree regularly, especially in the first year. If you do this, the roots will be strong and healthy, and the tree will be able to grow big and strong.
